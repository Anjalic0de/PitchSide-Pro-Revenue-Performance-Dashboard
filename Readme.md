# ⚽ PitchSide Pro — Executive Revenue Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

PitchSide Pro is an executive-focused Power BI dashboard created as part of the **Microsoft Fabric Data Days — DataViz World Championship Round 1 challenge**.

The objective of this project was to analyze:

**"What is driving changes in revenue performance over time, and where should the business focus next to sustain growth?"**

The dashboard transforms business data into actionable insights by analyzing revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, channel contribution, and growth opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

PitchSide Pro is a global football merchandise e-commerce company experiencing growth through:

- Seasonal demand
- Major football events
- Product launches
- Digital channel expansion

The challenge was to identify the key factors influencing revenue performance and provide recommendations for sustainable growth.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Revenue Overview
- Overall business performance summary
- Total Revenue, Profit, Orders, and Margin KPIs
- Revenue trend analysis
- Channel performance overview

### 2. Revenue Drivers Analysis
- Revenue changes over time
- Country-wise revenue contribution
- Channel performance comparison

### 3. Product Performance Analysis
- Top revenue-generating products
- Category-wise revenue distribution
- Revenue vs Profit analysis

### 4. Customer & Conversion Analysis
- Customer segment contribution
- Traffic and conversion analysis
- Acquisition channel performance

### 5. Business Insights & Recommendations
- Key findings from analysis
- Growth opportunities
- Strategic recommendations for improving performance

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Identified major contributors to revenue growth across channels and regions.
- Analyzed product categories based on revenue and profitability.
- Evaluated customer segments and conversion opportunities.
- Developed recommendations to support data-driven business decisions.
